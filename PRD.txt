# OpenBuilder — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# The Visual AI-Powered Builder Ecosystem for Student Innovators

---

# 1. Executive Summary

## Product Name

OpenBuilder

---

## Tagline

Build publicly. Collaborate openly.

---

## Product Description

OpenBuilder is a visual AI-powered platform where student developers, designers, founders, hackers, creators, and innovators can:

* showcase projects
* build public builder identities
* discover collaborators
* form hackathon teams
* document progress publicly
* receive AI startup guidance
* create professional portfolios
* continue projects after competitions

The platform combines:

* visual project showcasing
* developer portfolios
* startup execution tools
* AI assistance
* social discovery
* collaboration systems
* hackathon networking

into a single ecosystem designed specifically for ambitious student builders.

Unlike traditional productivity tools or portfolio websites, OpenBuilder focuses on:

* visibility
* builder identity
* collaboration
* execution
* startup culture
* community-driven innovation

OpenBuilder is designed to become:

> the internet home for the next generation of builders.
